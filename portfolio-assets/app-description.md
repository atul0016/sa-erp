## Short Description

SA ERP is a modern business management platform designed for Indian companies to manage accounting, inventory, sales, purchase, manufacturing, GST workflows, HRM, and reporting from a single desktop application.

## Portfolio Description

SA ERP is a full-scale ERP application built to simplify day-to-day operations for Indian businesses. The product combines finance, stock control, sales invoicing, purchase workflows, GST compliance, manufacturing operations, payroll, and reporting inside one clean desktop experience. It is designed to reduce process fragmentation and give business teams a faster, more structured way to run operations.

The system was built with Electron, React, TypeScript, and SQLite, making it suitable for local-first business environments where reliability, speed, and operational control matter. From chart of accounts and journal entries to invoice workflows, stock movement, GST summaries, and management reports, SA ERP is positioned as a practical ERP foundation for SMEs and growing companies.

## Long Case Study Description

SA ERP is a multi-module enterprise resource planning application created to centralize the core business workflows that Indian organizations typically manage across disconnected spreadsheets, accounting tools, and manual processes. The project focuses on operational simplicity, clean information structure, and role-oriented navigation so users can move between finance, inventory, sales, procurement, manufacturing, compliance, and HR without friction.

The application includes modules for chart of accounts, journal entries, general ledger, trial balance, customer and vendor workflows, GST-aware invoice management, stock movement, warehouse tracking, manufacturing operations, payroll, and report generation. The interface has been designed with a cleaner and more professional visual language to make enterprise workflows feel easier to handle while still looking polished enough for production-facing business software.

From a technical perspective, SA ERP uses Electron for desktop delivery, React with TypeScript for the interface layer, and SQLite for local data storage. This architecture makes the application suitable for offline-friendly or controlled on-premise environments while still allowing a strong modular front-end structure. For a portfolio, SA ERP demonstrates product thinking, business domain modeling, UI system design, desktop app architecture, and end-to-end feature planning across complex ERP domains.
