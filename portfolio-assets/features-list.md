# SA ERP Features List

## Core Product Positioning

- Unified ERP platform for Indian business operations
- Desktop-first architecture for controlled and local-first usage
- Modular information system covering finance, inventory, sales, purchase, manufacturing, HRM, GST, and reporting
- Clean operational UI designed for daily business workflows

## Finance Module

- Chart of accounts management
- Journal entry management
- General ledger view
- Trial balance reporting
- Profit and loss reporting
- Balance sheet reporting
- Financial structure designed for double-entry accounting flows

## Inventory Module

- Item master management
- Warehouse and location structure
- Stock movement tracking
- Stock valuation views
- Low stock alerts
- Batch and serial oriented inventory support in the domain model

## Sales Module

- Customer management
- Sales order workflows
- Sales invoice management
- GST-compliant invoice handling
- Customer receipt tracking
- Aging analysis for collections

## Purchase Module

- Vendor management
- Purchase order workflows
- Goods receipt note support
- Purchase invoice management
- Vendor payment tracking
- Aging analysis for payables

## Manufacturing Module

- Bill of materials support
- Work center structure
- Production order workflows
- MRP planning support
- Job work handling
- Quality control inspection support

## HRM Module

- Employee records
- Attendance workflows
- Leave management
- Payroll processing
- Tax declaration support

## GST and Compliance

- E-invoice support
- E-way bill support
- GSTR reporting structure
- ITC reconciliation support
- HSN summary reporting

## Reports and Analytics

- Financial report templates
- Sales reporting
- Purchase reporting
- Inventory reporting
- GST reporting
- Parameterized report generation with export options

## User Experience Highlights

- Cleaner navigation hierarchy
- Professional dashboard layout
- Search-first header experience
- Simple module access from sidebar
- Consistent card and action system
- Smooth visual transitions and refined workspace styling

## Technical Highlights

- Electron desktop architecture
- React and TypeScript front-end
- SQLite-backed local data model
- Modular IPC handler structure
- Multi-domain ERP service layer

## Portfolio-Worthy Value Proposition

- Demonstrates full product design across multiple enterprise domains
- Shows ability to translate business workflows into software modules
- Combines UI design, architecture, and technical implementation in one system
- Suitable as a flagship portfolio project for product engineering or full-stack roles
